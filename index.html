About Venus Industries

With 33 years of experience in the automotive industry, Venus Industries has established itself as a leading supplier of premium motorcycle spare parts. We are a Government recognized export house that specializes in the export of high-quality components for motorcycles and three-wheelers including both OEM and after market spare parts.

Our extensive product range includes genuine parts for popular brands like Bajaj, TVS, and other leading manufacturers. We are committed to providing our international clients with reliable, durable, and cost-effective solutions for their automotive needs.

Over the last few years Venus Industries is expanding, and touching new milestones across Africa and Asia, with our recent turnover nearing 5M USD in FY 24-25.
